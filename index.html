Jin.

Jin is the oldest BTS member, although he doesn’t often act like it. He’s known for his dad jokes, describing himself as “Worldwide handsome” in response the the times his pictures have gone viral, and winking or blowing kisses to the camera whenever it lands on him.

Suga.

Suga is the second oldest BTS member, and is often described as the dad of the group because he takes care of things around the dorm for the others. He’s known for having a demeanor that initially seems cold until you dig a little deeper. The other members like to joke about how he prefers to help them without anyone noticing. And when he gets really excited his silly side will shine right through.

J-Hope.

J-Hope is one of the best dancers in the group, and also also a literal sunshine angel!.J-Hope is known for being quite silly pretty much wherever he is because he likes to help others laugh and have a good time. If you watch him in any interview, he will most definitely introduce himself as “I’m your hope, you’re my hope, I’m J-Hope.” If you’re ever in a bad mood, all you need to do is watch some J-Hope videos and your day will get 100% better.

RM.

RM is the leader of BTS, and it’s rumored that he was the only surviving member of the original BTS lineup before they switched from an all Rap group to an Idol group. He played a big part in helping Big Hit finalize the group that fans get to enjoy today.

Jimin.

Jimin had the shortest amount of time as a trainee before BTS debuted, and almost didn’t make the cut. Although he’s a formally trained dancer, he had no experience with singing and had to continue to train hard over the years on his vocals. These days, he seems a lot more satisfied with his skills and has no problem hitting all the right notes live.

V.

V, also known as Taehyung, Tae, or TaeTae was the last member of BTS to be revealed. He actually didn’t even mean to audition for Big Hit but had joined a friend for moral support and was convinced by a staff member to try out as well.
